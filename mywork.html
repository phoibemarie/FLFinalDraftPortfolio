Visual Arts

With a strong focus on leveraging art and nature to deepen self-awareness, well-being, and access to justice, Faith is deeply passionate about engaging these domains as valuable sources of knowledge. She believes language is a form of empowerment and conscious raising. Her poetic form focuses on personhood, heritage, and observation. She has performed at open mics and community events including the 2023 Juneteenth Festival in Cincinnati and the 2023 National Underground Freedom Center. Faith Marie has also completed a performing art apprenticeship with Artwork V2 Gallery in September 2023.
